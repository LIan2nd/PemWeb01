Design
Design is the process of creating or planning something that serves a specific purpose or function. Design is often applied to products, environments, systems, or services to meet human needs and improve quality of life.
Typers of Design
There are several types of design, such as graphic design, interior design, product design, architectural design, and others.
Design typically involves several stages such as research, ideation, prototyping, and evaluation.
What Designer do
Designers must consider various factors such as aesthetics, ergonomics, technical, and cultural in their design process.
More
Design is also often considered an art that requires creativity and the ability to communicate a message through visual form.
